Context of the Project

Laurel Legenza, Comparative Health Systems Global Pharmacy Fellow, is leading this project under the mentorship of Warren Rose and Susanne Barnett, associate professors (CHS) in the Pharmacy Practice Division at the UW-Madison School of Pharmacy. This interactive visualization was created by Natalee Desotell with guidance from Professor Robert Roth, as a capstone project for the UW-Madison GIS Certificate Program. The data were provided by Erik Munson, Ph.D., D(ABMM).

The Data

This map is based on 2015 E. Coli data. The data were averaged within Wisconsin's Emergency Response Zone level (7 regions), but we have chosen to display it by county so that counties can be easily identified.
